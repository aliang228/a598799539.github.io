Autonomous driving

DeepRoute.ai launched affordable self-driving solution

DeepRoute.ai, Yunqi Partners’ portfolio company, announced DeepRoute-Driver 2.0, a production-ready Level 4 self-driving solution priced at under $10,000 USD, less than Tesla's full self-driving solution.

The innovative, series-production-ready L4 system includes five solid-state LiDAR sensors, eight cameras, and a self-developed computing platform integrated with its proprietary inference engine.

DeepRoute.ai is the only L4 self-driving company in China that has received funding from top Internet companies. And it focuses on the development and application of L4 self-driving technology. Yunqi Partners has been involved in the company’s seed funding stage since 2019 and offered support along the way. The current worth of the company has exceeded 1 billion USD.

Leadership; Autonomous driving

Cost Down is key to the self-driving solution: CEO of DeepRoute.ai interviewed by Forbes China

The keys to the company's success are cost down and to begin with the end in mind, said Guang Zhou, CEO of DeepRoute.ai, during an interview with Forbes China.

Technology is the company's core advantage that has allowed it to be the only company in China able to lower the price of DeepRoute-Driver 2.0, the company's flagship self-driving solution, to under $10,000 USD, less than Tesla's full self-driving solution.

And the technological advancement came from beginning with the end in mind. "Many people did not understand and questioned me in the beginning," Zhou said. "But, I proved this technology is a forward-looking plan. When it comes down to it, technology is based on thinking about the end game, because what self-driving ultimately requires is a low-cost, highly automated solution."

He is thankful for his years at Tsinghua University studying mathematics, physics and intellectual intelligence, and the precious experience in self-driving he gained in Baidu. Yunqi Partners has always been focused on investments in smart driving technologies and has invested in the angel round funding of DeepRoute.ai, which is now estimated to worth more than one billion USD.
